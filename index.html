Privacy Policy

TeachFlow  ·  Last updated: May 2025

Overview

TeachFlow is a classroom assistant app designed to help teachers manage their daily tasks. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

TeachFlow does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require account registration or login of any kind. All data you enter remains stored locally on your device only.

iCloud Sync

TeachFlow uses Apple's iCloud service to sync your data across your personal devices. This data is stored in your own private iCloud account and is managed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your iCloud data at any time. You can manage or disable iCloud sync at any time through your device's iCloud settings.

Third-Party Services

TeachFlow does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services.

Subscriptions

TeachFlow offers optional in-app subscriptions managed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. For subscription management, cancellation, or refunds, please visit your Apple ID settings or Apple Support.

Children's Privacy

TeachFlow is intended for use by teachers and educators. It is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.
